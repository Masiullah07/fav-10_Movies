My Top 10 Favorite Movies

The Shawshank Redemption

A story of hope and friendship between two prison inmates.

It's a powerful tale of redemption and human resilience.

The Godfather

The epic saga of a crime family and its rise to power.

Brilliant storytelling and iconic performances.

Kantara

Kantara is a 2022 Kannada film about a tribal community's beliefs, rights, and conflicts with the state. The film's main plot focuses on how their local deity protects them.

Rishab returns to his coastal Karnataka roots to present a tale that brings together nature, people, and the divine.

Mersal

The story of two estranged brothers, Dr. Maaran and Vettri, who come together to find their common enemy, Daniel.

The scenes where Thalapathy controls his anxiety when his wife (Nithya Menen) struggles to deliver Vetri are commendable.

Billa

The film follows the titular criminal's lookalike who replaces the severely wounded crime boss after a high-speed chase.

Billa seems to be a well-packaged action thriller.

Soorarai pottru

Nedumaaran Rajangam, also known as "Maara", a former Indian Air Force pilot who dreams of starting a low-cost airline to make air travel accessible to the common man

Inspired by the life events of Captain GR Gopinath, Sudha Kongara has come up with a beautiful tale of hope and triumph.

Hridayam

Arun's emotional journey from his college days to his life as a married man.

the film is "a fun-filled coming-of-age drama set in a typical engineering college campus; of love, break-ups, heartaches and the discovery of life in the process, also spilling over to the next phase.

Premalu

Sachin, an engineering student who falls in love with a girl named Reenu in college but is too shy to express his feelings.

'Premalu' is an enjoyable watch on the lives of young adults easing into their first jobs and discovering the recently-attained independence of being away from home.

Dear Comrade

A student union leader and a state-level cricketer who fall in love, but their relationship is threatened by his anger issues and violent behavior.

A love story involving an angry boy and a state level cricketer whose lives are totally different and they crosses their journey along with the issues of career and personal behaviour .

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge

A Bollywood romantic comedy about Raj (Shah Rukh Khan) and Simran (Kajol) who fall in love while traveling Europe on an inter-rail holiday.

Aditya showed us what falling in love, respect, and family values are all about.
